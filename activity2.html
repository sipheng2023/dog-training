On 19 August 2023 Dog Training and Security Center organized the first dog show event called International Dog Show Champaign in Laos. We have completed the dog show documents in accordance with the regulations of Lao PDR.

Thank you CP Company for the dog food Smartheart to our event support.

There are 2 kinds of competition events:

  • Beauty Dog
  • Knowledge Dog

The beauty Dog who wins is Loger (Loger from competitors participate and teach by Ms. Lee)

The knowledge Dog who wins is Coco (Coco from our center and teach by Teacher Inthaninh)

The event was full of new experiences, learning new things, facing many challenges and filled with joy for dog owners and dogs who came to play and watch the show. The purpose of the event is to showcase not only the beauty and diversity of canine breeds, but also to enhance the quality and opportunities of Lao society to meet international standards.
